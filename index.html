Happiness And Alcohol Consumption In 2016

These visualizations shows the alcohol consumption for virous region and how happy each region is.

HDI, Happiness Score and wine consumed

The higher HDI (Human Dvelopement Index) always means higher Happiness Score. The people in countries with higer HDI and happniess Score consume more wine. For example, Australian with HDI 938 and Happiness Score 7.313 drinks 212 ml wine everyday in average.

Pure Alcohol Consumption

Generally speaking, Europe is the region that consume the most alcohol worldwide. Russian people drink 11.5 Liter of pure alcohol a year, although they are not top consumer of wine, they are famous for drinking Spirits like vodka.

Happiness Score for Regions

Australia and New Zealand are considered to be the happiest region with happiness score 7.3. Sub-Saharan Africa with lowest score 4.2. Intrestingly, Central and eastern Europe only has score 5.4, significantly lower than the other high scored western regions.

Average Alcohol Consumption Daliy

The people in Europe regions, North America, Australia anz New Zealand drinks much more alcohol daily than the other area. People in more developed regions can afford and consume more alcohol.

Mattrix of Wine, Spirit Consumption and Happiness Score

In the spirit against happiness score plot(No.8), the shape is like a bell curve. The countries with middle(4-6) score consumes significantly more spirit han high(6-10) and low(0-4) scored countries. From all the plots included wine, can tell that wine is not very popular in many countries.
